Valheim - Ali-A

Valheim in a survival game in which you have to gather resources, level up and build equipment and many more. Every biome has a boss in which you have to defeat. There is 7 biomes in total in the game meaning that there are 7 bosses in total in order to beat the game as a whole. This can also be played with friends in which will make the gaming experience even more fun. I highly recommend this game if your into survival games especially if your looking a game to play with friends. Price: $20. Rating: 10/10.

Players go against each other or you can even play with a team and friends. In the game plalyers are able to hold on to each other in which can cause other players to lose in the races and minigames. I highly recommend this game if you want to play a chaos game with minigames and obstacles. Price: Free. Rating: 8/10.
